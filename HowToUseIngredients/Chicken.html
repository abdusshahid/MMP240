Baked Lemon Chicken with Garlic Lemon Cream Sauce | Baked Lemon Chicken Recipe

First, you will neen all the ingredients: 4 lbs chicken thighs (bone in), 1 & 1/2 lbs baby potatoes, 2 medium onions, 1 large lemon, 8 oz chicken broth. Watch the Video below! It will show you step by step how to make it.

Second, you need to season the Chicken: 1/2 tsp paprika, 1 tsp onion powder, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp lemon pepper, 1 tsp salt (or seasoning salt).

Third, you need to do for Cream Sauce: 1 tbls butter, 5 to 6 cloves minced fresh garlic, 1/2 tsp dried parsley, 1/4 tsp dried dill, 8 oz chicken broth, 1/4 cup lemon juice (fresh squeezed for best results), 16 oz heavy cream (full fat cream).
